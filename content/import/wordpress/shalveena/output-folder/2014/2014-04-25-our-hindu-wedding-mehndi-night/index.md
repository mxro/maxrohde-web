Her task: to make the popcorn that we will be using for the wedding ceremony (my brother will need to throw that popcorn into the holy fire as we do our rounds around the fire).

Here is Sharlene all prepared to fry the popcorn, with a big smile on her face and a big spatula in her hand haha. I was unfortunately not able to see all these things as I was strictly told not to leave my room upstairs!
